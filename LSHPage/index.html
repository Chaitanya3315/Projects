LIFE SPRING FOUNDATION

The LifeSpring Foundation is a non-profit organisation Promoted by LifeSpring Hospitals and registered as a Society in 2010 in Hyderabad.
LifeSpring Foundation’s Mission is to sponsor poor women who cannot afford pregnancy and childbirth at private healthcare service providers and facilitates childbirth at free of cost.

CONTRIBUTE

SERVICES

Birth sponsorships

Through Birth sponsorship programme LifeSpring Foundation collects donations from generous people and institutions to provide free and safe deliveries to the needy women identified either by Foundation or by the sponsors.

Access to High Quality & Dignified Childbirth

Foundation facilitates childbirth to the needy women at our promotor’s own hospital facilities in the name of “LifeSpring Hospitals”.
LifeSpring Hospitals in turn was promoted by HLL Lifecare Ltd., A Govt. of Indian Undertaking with an objective to provide high quality and dignified deliveries at an affordable cost to the low- income women from December 2005.

Childbirth in your neighbourhood

LifeSpring Hospitals, who is our partner for providing maternal health care services at a concessional cost, has a presence with its hospitals in ten different locations in twin cities of Hyderabad and all these hospital facilities are established in the neighbourhood areas where low-income and middle-class families reside.

Paybacks of Birth Sponsorship Donations

Ultilisation of Donations

You can donate Rs.21,000/- to sponsor one childbirth and the sponsored women will deliver with free of cost at the designated location of LifeSpring Hospital chosen by you in Hyderabad.
Otherwise, you can donate any amount or partial amount towards sponsoring births and multiple donations like this will be used to sponsor one childbirth.

gratitude to donors

Foundation facilitates for the Sponsors to meet the women, who get the benefit of Birth sponsorship, while the women is in the hospital for delivery / childbirth. It gives immense pleasure for the sponsors to interact directly with the beneficiary as well as for the sponsored woman to express her gratitude to the sponsoring donor.
Foundation also shares a video from the woman beneficiary with the sponsoring donor expressing her gratitude.

Income Tax Excemption under Sec.80G

Donation to LifeSpring Foundation is exempt U/s 80G as granted by Director of Income Tax Exemptions vide., F. No. DIT(E)/ITA No.1353/HYD/12/12-13 dated 21-08-2013, U/s 80G (5) of Income Tax Act and valid with effect from 21-08-2013 onwards.
